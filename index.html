Mr. & Mrs. Gamboa

The best parents evahhh

Here's a time line of Mr. & Mrs. Gamboa:

  • 1994 - At the age of 22-23, they got married at Barangay Labuan, San Quintin, Pangasinan
  • 1996 - Had their first born, Joana Rose Gamboa and moved to Baguio City.
  • 1997 - Joseph Gamboa went to Taiwan and worked as a construction worker for 3 years.
  • 2000 - Had their second born, Jullie Anne Gamboa.
  • 2003 - Had their third child, Jessica Reighn Gamboa.
  • 2006 - They had their first business, a small karinderya and water refilling station at Lourdes Dominican, Baguio City.
  • 2009 - Their unico hijo was born named, Mark Jaden Gamboa.
  • 2010 - They decided to buy a jeepney for extra income and Joseph Gamboa drove it for 10 years.
  • 2013 - They decided to close their small business.
  • 2016 - They had their first achievement. Their first daughter graduated
  • 2019 - The pandemic came. Joseph Gamboa was unable to work and they had financial crisis. He looked for side-line jobs.
  • 2021 - They sold the jeepney because it is already phased out. They used the money to buy a car and have another business called pasabuy vegetables to our province.
  • 2022 - Their second daughter graduated from college and stopped their pasabuy business. Joseph Gamboa is now working as a bus driver.
